The Band

Meet Giacomo Messina

Since the first time Bruno Mars touched the stage Giacomo knew that he wanted to be a part of his band... sadly that dream was ruined when he was not hired for his tours. Instead of being too upset about it he instead chose to join the "Almost Bruno Band" spending his days practicing and impersonating everything about the music. (He secretly hopes to truly make it to Bruno's band one day but this gig is close enough I guess...)

Meet Taeun Kim "Almost Anderson .Paak"

Since he was young, Taeun spent most of his time playing drums and singing, Unfortunately, despite all the hours of practice, his skills never quite caught up with his ambition, and he somehow earned the nickname “almost Anderson .Paak.” Realizing that joining Bruno Mars’ actual band was probably out of reach, he decided to aim a little lower and joined the “Almost Bruno Band” instead. He does not like it.
